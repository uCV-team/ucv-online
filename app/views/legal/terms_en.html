1.3 The Core Service (free)

PubliCV can offer a wide array of services. Among these, the core service hereby defined will always be free and unrestricted (except in the event of abuse).  

The core service is defined as follows:

  1. The user can create a publiCV  inclusive of their skills, professional experience, training and professional goals.
  2. A search engine is provided to select publiCVs with the desired attributes.
  3. Each publiCV provides a form to manually contact one candidate at a time.

The source code of the core service is open source and fully available here under MIT license: https://github.com/publicv-org/publicv .

Please note that the source code other than the one needed to provide the core service could not be open source and/or distributed with a different license.

1.4 Business Model

The business model of PubliCV, hence the paid services, will be built around the core service, hence, leaving the core service free of charge. An example of paid service built

 around the main one would be the ability to contact many candidates at the same time, provided they are selected by an advanced search criteria, sending the same message to all of them. In  this case the need to protect them from spam and other abuses arises. One solution is to offer this kind of service as a paid service, with a reasonable fee.
